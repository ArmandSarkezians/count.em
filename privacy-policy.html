count.em — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Overview

count.em is a calorie and nutrition tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data Storage

All food logs, nutrition goals, and personal settings are stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed into iCloud, your data is automatically synced to your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. This enables your data to persist across app reinstalls and sync between your devices. Apple handles the encryption and storage — we never have access to your iCloud data.

Data Deletion

You can delete all local app data by deleting the app. To also remove synced data from iCloud, go to Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → count.em → Delete Data.

Camera

count.em uses your device's camera to scan food barcodes and to capture photos for AI-based nutrition estimation. Camera access is only activated when you explicitly use the barcode scanner or photo capture features. Images and barcode data are processed on-device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Open Food Facts

When you scan a barcode, the app sends the barcode number to the Open Food Facts API to retrieve product nutrition data. No personal information is included in these requests beyond the barcode number.

USDA FoodData Central

When you search for foods or scan a barcode not found in Open Food Facts, the app queries the USDA FoodData Central API to retrieve nutrition data. Only the search query or barcode number is sent. No personal information is included in these requests.

On-Device AI

The AI nutrition estimation feature uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models. All processing happens locally on your device. No food descriptions or images are sent to external servers for this feature.

Third-Party Services

count.em does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not collect, share, or sell your personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

count.em is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
